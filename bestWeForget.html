Best we Forget

English Sonnet 1, December 19, 2017

The soul of man desires something to grasp
It shall decline thy treat of lone and pain
Yet why does thy him force upon the clasps?
Gents have nothing none more for thee to gain!
Then man falls down one dreams that he can feel
The loveth shines his dear calling from far
So far it flees but why from man it reels?
For no it stays but man is dragged afar
Yes, look one falls not sleep but death itself
His dreams a life which he shan’t ever get
His death upon not bed but earth’s flat shelf
His beloved widowed and left for dead
But as the lost are mourned uprise follows
And now the fading man is a sorrow

Awlan Stranger
