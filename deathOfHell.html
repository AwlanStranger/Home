Death of Hell

Unorthodox Sonnet 2, December 19, 2017

With the sword of the fallen, I present
The death of all that is righteous and pure
Before you stand those who you now resent
For they mann the gates of hell and your cure
Scratchy, raised voices, coloured crimson
Praise the gods of war that coloured them so
Their dying light lit aflame by arson
Hellish, darkness of intent forward go
And out they scream with all but voice and tongue
Perjury! Perjury! Guilty, guilty!
Over the corse you had stood, proud and tall,
While the body was rolled like swine in dung
False soil for such true noble dynasty
Such love for homicide is sure to fall

Awlan Stranger
